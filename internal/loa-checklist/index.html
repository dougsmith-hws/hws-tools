Initial contact by phone or text. Set intake call or send application link. Log in FUB immediately.

Next Day
Docs Requested
If docs not received, follow up. Don't let it go past 2 days without contact.
1–2 Days
Pre-Approved, Searching
Weekly check-in. Ask about search progress, any offers, questions. Keep them engaged.
Weekly
Under Contract

Weekly + Milestones
Nurture / Not Ready
Individual cadence based on their timeline. Could be monthly, quarterly. Note specific situation in FUB.

Closed Client

Annually + Triggered

Fast vs Slow Leads
Fast Lead 🔴
Actively searching, ready to apply now, has a property in mind, recent credit pull. Call same day — these leads go cold fast.
Same Day
Slow Lead 🟡
6–12 months out, credit work needed, lease not up yet. Establish rapport, set a future check-in date, let them know you're here when ready.
