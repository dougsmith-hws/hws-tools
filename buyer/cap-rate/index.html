7 Keys of Profitable Real Estate Investment
1. Leverage — Is (cap rate + appreciation) > mortgage rate?
2. Rate of Return — Cash on Cash + IRR analysis (see IRR Calculator)
3. Liquidity — Real estate equity is NOT liquid. Maintain cash cushion. Larger mortgage = more liquidity.
4. Marketability — Brokerage fees, advertising, title, transfer taxes = "cost to carry" when you exit.
5. Management — Asset management (performance vs. opportunities) + property management (maintenance, tenants).
6. Tax Impact — Tax credits, depreciation deductions, 1031 exchanges, passive vs. active income classification.
7. Risk — Vacancy, lease expiration, tenant credit, increasing carry costs. Mitigate via rent-to-own, diversification, cash cushion.
