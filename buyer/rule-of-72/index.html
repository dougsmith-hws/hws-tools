The Rule of 72

At 7.0%, your $50,000 doubles to $100,000 in approximately 10.3 years. Simply divide 72 by the rate — no calculator needed. It's a quick mental shortcut used by investors and advisors everywhere.

Applied to real estate: A home appreciating at 4% doubles in value in approximately 18 years. Your down payment — the leveraged piece — can grow even faster as a percentage return because you control the full asset value with a fraction of it in equity. A $50,000 down payment on a $350,000 home that appreciates to $700,000 represents a 13× return on your actual cash invested, even though the property only doubled.
