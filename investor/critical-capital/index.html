Critical Capital is the lump sum a client must accumulate to generate their desired retirement income indefinitely — without depleting principal. Social Security or pension income reduces the portfolio income required, directly lowering the target.
Critical Capital = (Desired Income − SS/Pension) ÷ (Return Rate − Inflation Rate)
Example: ($100,000 − $24,000 SS) ÷ (6% − 2%) = $1,900,000 needed.
